Product Overview

A Holistic K-12 Platform Powered by Cognitive Memory

EdwithAI is a holistic K-12 education platform powered by a three-layer cognitive memory system that gives every child their own AI tutor — one that remembers how they learn, when they struggle, and what makes them light up.

Unlike generic AI tools that forget everything after each conversation, EdwithAI builds a living portrait of every student: their strengths, their pace, their emotional state, and their journey. Teachers get behavioral intelligence. Parents get peace of mind. And children get an experience that feels less like software and more like a patient, adaptive mentor who never gives up on them.

What Makes EdwithAI Unique

Eight core capabilities that transform how children learn, how teachers teach, and how parents stay connected.

🧠

The AI That Remembers

Most AI tutors have amnesia. EdwithAI's three-layer cognitive memory changes that.

Working Memory — tracks engagement, response times, and cognitive load in real-time.

Episodic Memory — captures breakthroughs, struggles, and fatigue events across sessions.

Semantic Memory — maps every concept with spaced repetition scheduling.

🎯

Adaptive Difficulty — Zero Guesswork

The Teacher Engine runs real-time behavioral analysis — mastery scores, consecutive answer patterns, fatigue detection — to compute the optimal difficulty level for every single question.

If a child is on a hot streak, the challenge increases. If they're struggling, the approach changes. If they're tired, the session wraps gently. All automatically.

📚

Your Textbooks Become AI-Searchable

Upload any textbook PDF or YouTube lecture, and EdwithAI's ingestion engine breaks it into searchable knowledge. The AI doesn't just answer from the internet — it answers from your curriculum.

This is Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) grounded in your syllabus, not Wikipedia.

💬

A Thinking Buddy, Not an Answer Machine

The Thinking Buddy teaches children how to think — through pattern recognition, decomposition, hypothesis testing, and analogy. Every reasoning step is transparent, showing the why behind every thought.

This isn't autocomplete for homework. It's a cognitive co-pilot that nurtures independent, holistic thinking.

🛡️

Child Psychology Monitoring — Built In

EdwithAI quietly analyses chat patterns using a validated AI psychology framework. It detects stress levels, emotional states, and wellbeing scores — surfacing high-alert situations to teachers and parents immediately.

Regular wellbeing reports give parents visibility into their child's emotional landscape, without being intrusive.

👨‍🏫

Teachers Get Superpowers

EdwithAI doesn't replace teachers — it amplifies them.

Classroom Intelligence Suite — behavioral insights across every student.

Lesson Planner — auto-generates plans tied to uploaded materials.

Assessment Manager — creates, distributes, and tracks assignments.

Live Classroom — real-time virtual teaching.

Behavioral Alerts — flags students who need attention early.

🎮

Gamification That Actually Works

EdwithAI tracks XP, streaks, badges, and leaderboards — but unlike bolted-on gamification, these are wired into the adaptive engine.

A student's streak feeds into reminder messages, difficulty adjustments, and the AI's encouragement. When a child breaks a streak, the AI knows how to bring them back without pressure.

🔌

Provider-Agnostic AI — No Lock-In

Schools aren't locked into one AI vendor. EdwithAI supports Google Gemini, OpenAI GPT, and Anthropic Claude — switchable per school via a configuration panel.

No code changes. No migration headaches. If a better model launches tomorrow, your school can switch in seconds.
